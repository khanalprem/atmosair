Excel your drone operations

Windsock BVLOS is a customized decision support tool that combines high-resolution weather forecasts with powerful en-route flight safety algorithms. We build it to:

  • Help drone operators fly BVLOS mission as safely as possible.
  • Be legally compliant with regards to preflight weather checks.

  • The high-resolution forecasts are resolved at anywhere between 1.3 to 1.8km spatial resolution (a sub-mile resolution).

    The proprietary flight safety algorithm is customizable by each operator based on their operational requirements and their internal safety procedures.

Windsock BVLOS allows you to:

  • Input the flight performance data of each UAV in the fleet, to obtain a model-specific safety evaluation for each route.
  • Plot and assess the route against different weather parameters, at fly over time.
  • Estimate the duration of the flight based on the input parameters and the forecasted wind conditions.
  • Check the feasibility of the mission with an easy traffic light feedback.

The goal was to take the complexity out of weather decisions. All this, in short, to keep your mind on what matters most: getting your job done.
